Workflow

Control how ticket assignments and status transitions behave

Auto-transition on assignment When enabled, assigning a ticket in the default "Open" status automatically moves it to "In Progress".
Auto-send ticket confirmation email When enabled, Kanzen emails the contact automatically as soon as a ticket is created from an inbound email. When disabled, agents trigger the confirmation manually from the ticket page.
Auto-assign inbound-email tickets to Agents When enabled, tickets created from inbound customer emails are routed to the Agent (role: Agent) with the fewest open tickets, ties broken by least-recently-assigned. Offline agents are skipped. When disabled, email tickets arrive unassigned and admins/managers triage them manually.
