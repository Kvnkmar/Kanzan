What you get

Everything your support team needs to actually know your customers.

No more flipping between Zendesk and HubSpot to answer a single question. Every ticket, every contact, every pipeline stage — together.

Unified inbox + CRM

Every ticket, every conversation, one contact record. When a customer emails, your team sees their full history without switching tabs.

SLA-aware contacts

See SLA status right on the contact card. Know which customers have urgent open tickets before you reply, not after.

Kanban pipeline

Move deals and support stages side by side. Visualize where every customer sits — sales, onboarding, or active support — on one board.

Contact timeline

Every customer interaction, in one chronological feed.

Email replies, support tickets, status changes, deal updates, and notes — stitched into a single timeline per contact. Your team scrolls one screen instead of digging through three tools.

  • Threaded ticket history with linked email replies
  • Full audit trail — who changed what, and when
  • Pin notes that stay visible above the fold
Round-robin assignment

Auto-assign tickets to whoever is least busy.

Kanzen tracks each agent's open ticket load and last-assigned timestamp, then routes new tickets to the agent with the lightest workload — fairly, automatically, and with full audit logs.

  • Workload-aware routing across queues and skills
  • Respects working hours and out-of-office
  • One-click manual override when needed
